About company

At the moment, the plant produces:

Turning group machines:

  • universal turning and screw-cutting machine of the GS526 model, designed to perform various turning operations in centers or a cartridge, as well as to cut threads. Fashion machines have been created on its basis. HS 526U, HS 526UM, HS 526U-01, HS 526UM-01, HS 526U-02, HS 526UM-02, etc., with the length of the part to be installed from 1000 to 2000 mm. Станки могут комплектоваться сверлильно-фрезерными устройствами, позволяющими дополнительно выполнять сверлильные, фрезерные работы и нарезание резьбы под разными углами на деталях, установленных на суппорте токарного станка;
  • lathes with UFD;
  • CNC lathes
  • units (feed boxes and aprons to turning-screw machines, heads automatic to CNC machines).

Drilling group machines:

  • gamma radial drilling machines (machines of model 2K522-03, GS545, 2K550V);
  • vertical drilling machine of model 2T140 (largest drilling diameter is 40mm);
  • gamma table drilling machines (machines of model GS2116K, GS2112, 2P118).

Key products

Screw-cutting lathe model GS526U highened accuracy

It is designed for performing a wide variety of turning operations, as well as for cutting metric, inch, modular and pitch threads. The machine is equipped with an apron with the built-in engine of the accelerated movement and the feed box giving possibility of cutting a carving of 11 and 19 threads on inch without replacement of replaceable gears in a transmission.

CNC lathe model 16GS25(32)F3C

The machine is designed for turning cartridge and center work in a closed cycle of processing parts of the type of rotation bodies with a stepped and curved profile in various industries in small-scale and serial production. The machine can be used for external turning, boring, drilling, threading according to the program.

CNC lathe model 16GS32(25)SU

The machine with the increased diameter of processing of a bar over a bed and a caliper It is designed for turning in a semi-automatic cycle in the chuck or centers of parts with a straight, stepped and curved profile in conditions of small-scale and serial production.

Radial drilling machine model 2K550V

The machine can be used for drilling, reaming, countersinking, reaming, and threading in steel, cast iron, and other metals and alloys, with the exception of those alloys whose processing on the machine can lead to a fire hazard.

CNC lathe model GS1750

The machine has an inclined bed layout and is available with a main spindle that can be optionally supplied with a spindle brake or a circular spindle feed positioning device (C-axis). It is equipped with a 12-position head for turning tools or turning and driving tools.
