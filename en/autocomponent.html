About company

A specialized company for the design and production of starters and generators for engines of trucks and cars, buses, agricultural equipment and special equipment. Among the regular consumers of the products are BELAZ - the managing company of the holding BELAZ-HOLDING, MINSK MOTOR PLANT, MAZ - the managing company of the holding BELAUTOMAZ, KAMAZ, Sollers, GAZ Group and many other organizations.

Companies including:
