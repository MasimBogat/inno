About company

Currently, the production program has about 180 basic elevator models:

  • passenger elevators with a carrying capacity of 225, 300, 320, 400, 500, 630, 1000 and 1275 kg with a speed of up to 2.0 m/s;
  • cargo elevators with a carrying capacity of 100, 250, 500, 1000, 2000, 3200, 5000 and 6300 kg;
  • hospital elevators with a carrying capacity of 500 kg, 630 kg, 1000 kg, 1275 kg, 1600 kg;
  • cottage elevators with a carrying capacity of 225 kg.
