For exact cutting of metal and non metallic sheet materials on the set contour. The maximum size of the processed sheet is 3x12m. maximum cutting speed 8000mm / min. Precision of cut parts – according to EN ISO 9013. The maximum thickness of the cut metal is up to 250mm

Technology and equipment for precision casting of gasified models

The technology has a number of advantages over traditional casting methods and it allows you to produce castings weighing from 10 g to 2 t with a surface purity of Ra 3.2-6.3, dimensional accuracy up to class 7 from carbon and alloy steels, gray, high-strength cast iron, bronze and brass, aluminum alloys

new composite magnetic material and products based on it

New magnetic material can replace laminated electromagnetic steel for high frequency applications in products such as transformers, chokes, modern high-speed valve motors and generators. The main advantage of the new material over electromagnetic steel is the absence of eddy current losses at high frequencies due to the electrical isolation of individual iron particles in the composite material
