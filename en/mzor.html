About company

Manufacturer of heavy multifunctional metal cutting machines and processing centers with CNC of various levels of automation for processing large-sized parts up to 5,000 mm wide and up to 30,000 mm long.

List of equipment developed and manufactured at OJSC MZOR:

  • portal heavy longitudinal milling and boring machines with movable table or movable portal;
  • horizontal drilling-milling-boring machines with NC and UACI;
  • single-stage horizontal longitudinal milling and boring machines with a movable stand or a movable table equipped with CNC and UACI systems;
  • NC and UACI vertical milling machines
  • longitudinal strict and strict-milling machines;
  • special edge-strict and edge-milling machines;
  • longitudinal grinding machines;
  • Long machines;
  • CNC turning and carousel machines and automatic tool change;
  • balancing machines of various degrees of automation;
  • two-stamp torphobricket presses;
  • wire and nail machines;
  • special milling machines for processing reinforcement of internal and external joints at the ends of pipes;
  • special process equipment designed and manufactured in accordance with the individual requirements of the Customer.
