About company

Enterprise for the production of modern vertical and horizontal processing centers.Currently, the plant produces:

  • Vertical cantilever milling machines FSS350MR, FSS450MR;
  • Horizontal cantilever milling machines with cross table FW350MR, FW450MR;
  • Horizontal cantilever milling machines with turntable FU350MR, FU450MR;
  • Wide-universal cantilever-milling machine FU350MRApUG, FU450MRApUG;
  • Vertical and horizontal cantilever milling machines with digital indication;
  • Vertical and horizontal cantilever milling machines with SPPU;
  • Vertical NC cantilever milling machines;
  • 3-axis vertical processing centers;
  • 5 axial vertical processing centers;
  • Horizontal processing centres;
  • Long machines;
  • Correct-cut machines.

Key products

3-axis vertical machining center BYVER500

BYVER500 has a number of advantages in comparison with the previously produced equipment: the layout solution is a movable three-axis module of the spindle head and a fixed table. Thus, the dynamics of the machine is not related to the mass of the workpiece; increased spindle speed up to 8,000 rpm, as well as its static and dynamic rigidity in order to ensure high-quality finishing of manufactured parts with increased accuracy characteristics; high positioning accuracy (± 4μm); increased speed of fast (installation) movements of the movable units of the machine up to 40 m / min in order to reduce the auxiliary processing time of parts; reduction of tool change time to 6 seconds in order to reduce the auxiliary processing time of parts; the use of advanced types of components and materials with improved technical and economic parameters can reduce the energy consumption of the machine.

Horizontal machining centers ГДМ630, BYHOR630

The horizontal drilling-milling-boring machining center is designed to create flexible productions in individual, small-scale and batch production, it allows the implementation of flexible production technology based on the trends of modern machine-tool industry and the best practices of leading foreign companies. The headstock is equipped with a high-speed (up to 10,000 rpm) electric spindle. This allows for high-speed machining of body parts made of ferrous and non-ferrous metals with high-strength carbide tools. Cutting speeds in steel and cast iron exceed 500m / min (compared to 70–250m / min in conventional machining) due to a decrease in torque with an increase in the working spindle speed and feed rate. The transition to high-speed machining allows increasing productivity in comparison with classical machining centers by 2-3 times, improving the quality of the machined surfaces, and reducing manual finishing operations. High-speed machining is characterized by a significant reduction in cutting forces, which is important when machining loose and thin-walled parts. In high speed milling, heat is quickly removed from the chip area, reducing heat and deformation of the part.

Широкоуниверсальный консольно-фрезерный станок мод. FU350MRApUG-03, FU450MRApUG

"Широкоуниверсальный консольно-фрезерный станок мод. FU350MRApUG-03, FU450MRApUG (аналоги, производимые в СССР — 6T82Ш, 6T83Ш). Широкоуниверсальные консольно-фрезерные станки производства ОАО «StankoGomel» позволяют вести фрезерование деталей из стали и чугуна как вертикальным шпинделем, так и горизонтальным.
