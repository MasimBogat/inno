About company

The leading manufacturer of modern diesel engines among the CIS countries ranks first in the number of tractor and combine engines produced.

The main areas of activity are:

  • production and marketing in the domestic market and abroad of diesel engines and their spare parts, aluminum casting, components, process equipment, commercial tools;
  • execution of design and engineering works;
  • production and sale of construction equipment, electric diesel generators, compressors, blowers;
  • production and marketing of consumer goods;
  • wholesale and retail trade in own goods, as well as in purchased goods;
  • foreign economic activity;
  • warranty and service services of products sold to consumers.
