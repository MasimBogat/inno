About company

The company, which produces products according to world quality standards - metal products, which include continuous cast billet, rolled stock, reinforcement rolled stock, seamless hot-rolled pipes for various purposes, metal cord, onboard armored wire, RML wire, wire for various purposes and steel fiber. The share of exports in the total shipment of products is about 85%. The total geography of exports in the 37-year history of the enterprise is 118 countries worldwide.
